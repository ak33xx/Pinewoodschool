History

“Learning gives creativity, Creativity leads to thinking, Thinking provides knowledge and Knowledge makes you great” APJ Abdul Kalam

01 May 1995 dawned as the day of reckoning & academic renaissance for village Hamirpur (J&K), when one of the first Army Goodwill Schools of J&K was established by Hamirpur battalion of the BG Brigade in the border district of Poonch on Line of Control. The dream of Col (later Maj Gen, Retd) KVS Lalorta, CO 2/11 GR, was realized with raising of the Pinewood School (PWS). The founding team also included Mrs Nandita Singh (CO’s wife), Capt Amitabh Tewari, unit medical officer, and Capt Sridharan, a TA officer, a Physics Professor from Loyola Junior College, Pune.

And since then, the school has never looked back, while making unbound progress over two & half decades to be one of the best schools in the area while endeavoring to illuminate hearts & minds of more than 400 students each year. The dream would not have come true, but for the sheer sacrifice & dogged determination of the raising pioneers and those who followed till now. Leading from the front, Mrs Nandita Singh left all her house hold responsibilities for two months; camped at Hamirpur to ensure hassle-free raising. The beginning of the journey was indeed humble, and the school started in two tents, before being adopted by the BG bde as one of the most promising future Op Sadbhavana projects.

About Pinewodd

At Pinewood School, we take pride in fostering an environment where students are encouraged to grow academically, socially, and emotionally.

The brand PWS has emerged as an educational standard trademark in the area, and locals, through their children want to associate with it. Since the infrastructure available permits only around 400 students, the expansion is on the cards. High quality education, discipline, and more importantly, the social & self-education imparted at PWS is a rare sight. HQ Northern Command, through Operation Sadbhavana has unconditionally supported the school financially for education of the poor & needy. Recent initiatives at hand include projects costing more than Rs 50 lacs like BPL scholarships, renovation/ addition of class rooms & toilets

The product of PWS has something special, possibly due to the disciplined education & grooming. The faculty takes upon itself the pious responsibility to ignite the young minds for unbound learning through quality education. The teachers not only cover academic syllabus, but also share learnings of life & the experience.

Our Aim

St. Clare’s school to be the powerful agent of transforming human beings and bringing about a just society, with qualitative and a value based education: thus creating individuals who are intellectually competent, morally sound, emotionally whole and who promote the values of God’s Kingdom where love , truth, peace , justice and harmony prevail.

Our Mission

The mission of the school, supported by the coordinated efforts of all stakeholders, is to provide quality education to all children in the area, without prejudice to caste, creed, color, religion, gender, or social status. The school aims to cultivate strong character, instilling values of discipline, hard work, respect for diverse religions and cultures, and a love for the environment. It strives to foster in students a genuine sense of patriotism, national unity, respect for human dignity, honesty, and sportsmanship.
