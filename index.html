About the School

Pinewood at a Glance

The slow, but steady journey starting in 1995, has achieved many a milestones, steadily & surely, built upon hard work of the Hamirpur battalion, teachers and cooperating parents. Within four years of raising, PWS was offered affiliation by J&K Education Board for classes up to Vth standard, extended to Class VII in 2002 and eventually reached to class X & XII in the years 2005 & 2009 respectively. In the pious journey, the school was extended all possible moral & material support by Indian Army as well as the local civil administration.

“Higher and Higher”

PWS inspires the students to excel through knowledge & learning.

Every morning, one can find Bhimber Gali road junction, crowded with large number of the Pinewoodian students, dressed immaculately, standing confidently, in wait for the army school buses. The body language and their demeanour speaks volumes about their self-discipline and the social education cum grooming ensured at the PWS. Seldom will they miss the school, because of the love and respect for the institution, which is the temple of their basic education and bright future.

Computer LabsTo Redefine the youth

Pinewood School offers state-of-the-art computer labs that give students hands-on experience with technology. These labs are equipped with the latest hardware and software, providing a safe and guided environment where students can learn essential computing skills, from basic programming to digital creativity.

Sports and GamesFostering our Students

At Pinewood, we understand that physical activity plays an essential role in fostering well-rounded development. By combining outstanding sports facilities with academic and technological excellence, we create an environment where students can grow, learn, and thrive in all aspects of life.
