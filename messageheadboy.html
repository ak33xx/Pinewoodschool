Head Boy's Message

I am honoured to serve as the Head boy for this academic year. As we embark on this journey together, I am excited about the opportunities and challenges that lies ahead. Our School community is known for its spirit of collaboration, respect and dedication to excellence and I am committed to upholding these values.

My goal is to foster an environment where every student feels supported and motivated to achieve their best. I encourage all of us to participate actively in School activities, embrace new ideas and contribute positively to our School community. Together, we can make this year memorable and impactful.

Head Girl's Message

“Success is not measured by the position you reach ,but by the obstacle you overcome to get there”

I would like to thank our respected principal, honourable vice principal, my teachers and all my friends for believing in me and giving me a chance to lead the school parliament. I promise to fulfill responsibilities to the best of my ability and constantly rise to meet the expectations vested in me. It is a privilege to serve as your Head Girl, and I want to take this moment to share a few thoughts that I hope will inspire and motivate each of you.

This year holds the promise of countless possibilities—opportunities to learn.I know that the path to success is not always easy , there will be challenges and there will be moments of doubt. But remember, it is through facing these challenges that we grow stronger and more resilient. Embrace every obstacle as a chance to learn and to improve, each setback is a stepping stone towards greater achievements. Believe in yourselves and your abilities. You are capable of more than you know. Whether it’s excelling in academics, participating in extracurricular activities, or supporting your peers, every effort you make contributes to our collective success. Your enthusiasm, dedication, and hard work are what will make this year truly remarkable.
