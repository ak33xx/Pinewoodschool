Principal Message

Dear Parents and Well-wishers, It gives me immense pleasure and joy to welcome you to Pinewood School and to introduce you to the wide range of educational opportunities offered both through the curriculum and the rich-varied realms of extracurricular activities. Pinewood School is committed to providing quality education with a great emphasis on instilling traditional values and virtues.

Our aim is to ensure that every student is regarded as a valuable person whose mind, body, and spirit are nurtured in a loving and caring environment. Pinewood School provides such a domain that brings focus and direction to the lives of students and prepares them for a bright future.

The school, with an enthusiastic, experienced, and highly qualified Teaching Staff, along with excellent infrastructure facilities, provides a holistic atmosphere for each and every child to develop into a capable and competent young wizard of today, with an extra measure of confidence and courage.

With a deep sense of appreciation and regard for each one of you,
